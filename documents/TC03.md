### TC03 - Remove Product from Cart

**Description**: Remove a product using the Remove button.

**Preconditions**: MacBook Pro is already in the cart

**Steps**:

1. Go to homepage and add MacBook Pro to cart
2. Open the cart
3. Click the Remove button for MacBook Pro
4. Verify the product is removed

**Expected Results**:

- MacBook Pro disappears from the cart
- Empty cart message is shown (if no other items)
