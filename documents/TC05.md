### TC05 - Update Product Quantity to 0 (Remove via Quantity)

**Description**: Set quantity to 0 to remove the product from cart.

**Preconditions**: MacBook Pro is in the cart.

**Steps**:

1. Open the cart.
2. Change quantity of MacBook Pro to 0.
3. Click the Update button.
4. Verify the product is removed.

**Expected Results**:

- MacBook Pro is no longer visible in the cart table.
- Empty cart message appears.
