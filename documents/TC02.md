### TC02 - Add Product to Cart

**Description**: Successfully add MacBook Pro to the shopping cart.

**Steps**:

1. Go to homepage
2. Search for "MacBook Pro"
3. Apply "In stock" filter
4. Click on the MacBook Pro product
5. Click "Add to Cart"
6. Open the cart (View Cart)

**Expected Results**:

- Success message appears
- MacBook Pro is visible in the cart table
