### TC01 - Verify Empty Cart

**Description**: Check that the cart shows empty state when no products are added.

**Preconditions**: Cart is empty

**Steps**:

1. Go to homepage
2. Click on the cart icon (showing "0")
3. Verify empty message in the cart drawer
4. Click "Edit cart"
5. Verify empty message on the main cart page

**Expected Results**:

- "Your shopping cart is empty!" message appears in both cart drawer and main cart page
