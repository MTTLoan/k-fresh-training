### TC04 - Update Product Quantity

**Description**: Update product quantity from 1 to 2.

**Preconditions**: MacBook Pro is in the cart with quantity 1

**Steps**:

1. Go to homepage and add MacBook Pro to cart
2. Open the cart
3. Change quantity of MacBook Pro to 2
4. Click the Update button
5. Verify success message
6. Check new quantity and total price

**Expected Results**:

- Success message: "You have modified your shopping cart!"
- Quantity shows "2"
- Line total shows "$4,000.00"
